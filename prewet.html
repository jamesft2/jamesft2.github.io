Prewet Phase Introduction

The pre-wet phase appears in biological system when there is not sufficient enthalpy to wet the surface (the polymers don't want to be near eachother) but they are still attracted to the surface. This creates a molecularly thin layer of polymer on the surface, which can give rise to interesting physics.
