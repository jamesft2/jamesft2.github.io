Journal Articles & Publications

Graduate Degree

  1. Simulating Curved Lipid Membranes Using Anchored Frozen Patches
    JF Tallman, A Statt, The Journal of Physical Chemistry B, 2025
    We present a method for simulating curved lipid membranes by anchoring frozen patches of lipids to create arbitrary curvature. This allows us to simulate out-of-equilibrium ensembles with imposed membrane curvature.
  2. Lipid Membrane Leaflets Unzip upon Hybridization with Polymer-Rich Nanodomains
    JF Tallman, N Kambar, C Leal, A Statt, Macromolecules 57 (24), 11688-11696, 2024
    We use molecular dynamics simulations to predict that large hydrophobic polymers can unzip lipid bilayers, which has implications for drug delivery and the design of hybrid lipid-polymer materials. This result is validated through experimental imaging.
